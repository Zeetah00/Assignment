THE ORIGIN OF THE NAME (ZITA)

The name Zita is girl's name of Greek origin meaning "little girl; seeker". The name may originate from the Italian word zitta meaning young girl or from the Hungarian pet name of Felicita, from Latin Felicia. In Basque, the word means saint. In Greek, the word means' the hunter'.
